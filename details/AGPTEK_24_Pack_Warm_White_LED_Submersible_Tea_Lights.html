24 Pack Warm White LED Submersible Tea Lights

Each unit comes with a built-in on/off switch and a CR2032 battery, making them ready to use straight out of the box. The long-lasting LED bulbs provide hours of continuous light, ideal for special occasions or everyday decorative use.

Whether it's a romantic dinner, a grand wedding, or a cozy party, these 24-pack LED tea lights are the perfect addition to your decor. Their waterproof and submersible features make them a versatile choice for both indoor and outdoor settings.
